IV Dengue Package

Bali’s tropical climate makes it a paradise for travelers — but also for mosquitoes. During the rainy season, the risk of dengue fever increases as these mosquitoes thrive and multiply. Dengue fever is a viral infection transmitted by the Aedes aegypti mosquito. It commonly causes high fever for 3–7 days, accompanied by headache, nausea, vomiting, joint pain, weakness, and sometimes a red rash on the arms and legs. While most cases are mild, dengue can become serious without proper care and hydration. If you experience symptoms of dengue fever during your stay in Bali, don’t hesitate to contact Cassa Medical Clinic for professional care.
